# Importing Game Film (Coach Guide)

There are two ways to get film into CoachLenz: paste a link (**Import from URL**) or
**Upload File**. Once film is imported, it is copied into your CoachLenz library
permanently, so it stays yours even if the original link later changes.

## No Hudl account? You can still import your film

You do **not** need your own Hudl account to bring in film. Pick whichever of these
is easiest for you.

### 1. Use Hudl's "Download" link (no login needed on our end)

This is the simplest path if the film lives on Hudl.

1. In Hudl, open the video.
2. Click **Download**.
3. Hudl emails you a download link.
4. Copy that link, go to CoachLenz → **Import Film** → **Import from URL**, and paste it.

That download link already contains the video file itself, so CoachLenz imports it
directly with no Hudl login. When the link is a Hudl download link, you will see a
green "no Hudl account needed" note confirming it will work.

> **Import it right away.** Hudl download links expire quickly (usually within
> minutes). If it fails, just generate a fresh download link in Hudl and paste the
> new one.

### 2. Upload the file

Works from any source, every time.

1. Save the video to your phone or computer.
2. Go to **Import Film** → **Upload File** and choose the video.

Supported: MP4, MOV, AVI, up to 20GB.

### 3. Use a free host

If you already have the film on a free service, just paste the share link:

- Google Drive
- Dropbox
- YouTube (an **unlisted** video works fine)
- Vimeo

Set the file's sharing to **"anyone with the link"**, copy the link, and paste it into
**Import from URL**. No Hudl account required.

## When a link asks for a login

Some Hudl and NFHS Network links point at a **watch page** that is private or
subscription-only. Those need a login that your device has but our server does not.
If that happens:

- Use one of the three no-account options above (a Hudl **Download** link, **Upload
  File**, or a free host), **or**
- Ask your CoachLenz admin to connect a Hudl account once, under
  **Connected Accounts** in the sidebar. After that, private team film imports with
  one click.

## A note on video quality

Jersey-number reading depends on resolution. Film below 720p (for example, a 360p
stream) makes numbers too small to read reliably, so player-level detail is limited.
Import in **720p or higher (HD)** whenever you can. A downloaded file or an HD upload
gives the best results.
